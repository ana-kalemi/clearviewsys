500 EUR bills denomination

In europe 500 euro notes are often used in money laundering, thus we are required to provide reporting on the use of any 500 euro notes for each transaction involving 500 Euro notes

Declaring acceptable denominations

We need to define all valid denominations of the currency. This is only done once. From the pull down menu select "Denominations".

Add all acceptable denominations. To add a denomination just enter the denomination figure and press enter.

These denominations are usually accepted at most exchange houses. Some may not accept the 2 Euro coins.

Accessing the Sanction Lists using your Web Browser

CurrencyXchanger uses a webservice developed by Clear View Systems to check for entities and individuals that are listed on government sanctions lists such as: OFAC SDN, OSFI, AUSTRAC, and New Zealand's Police. The OFAC and OSFI list get updated every automatically without human intervention.

The Sactions List online service

Open your favorite web browser (e.g. Safari, Explorer) Goto the site:
ewire.me/sanctions

The online service provides a central place to lookup a name.

As a customer of Clear View Systems in good standing, you can sign in to the online service by providing a ClientCode and ClientKey. The ClientCode/ClientKey are assigned to customers when they first purchase any of the CurrencyXchanger line of products. If you don't know you ClientCode/ClientKey, you can call or email Clear View Systems.

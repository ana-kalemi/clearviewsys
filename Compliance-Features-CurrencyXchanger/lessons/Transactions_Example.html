Searching for a customer in the system

When searching for a customer, the system shows all important information about the customer including picture ID, address, phone etc...

Denomination entry calculator

Use the + and * (asterisk) on your numeric KEYPAD to quickly enter the denominations, just like you are using a calculator. For example, enter 1*100+1*500+ and then press the ENTER key on the keypad to save denominations entry.

The result of the saved invoice

The denomination count is also shown on the invoice after your save the invoice.

We used John W. Howard as a customer and selling 12000 EUR as an example.

Even though most fields are entered for the customer, the SS is missing, and so is the source of funds and whether the customer is doing the transaction for a third party.

The expiry date is now fixed

Even though the SS in now entered in the system, the SOF (Source of Funds) and TPD (Third Party Determination) is not yet complete.

We need to specify that the teller asked about any third party involvements. These rules are set in the AML Rules. When you check the asked about 3rd party checkbox, type the result in the 3rd party field even it there was no third party. Leaving a 3rd party field blank is not good practice.

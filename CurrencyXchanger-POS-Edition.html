What is CurrencyXchanger POS? | Multi-Currency Point Of Sale Software

Over the course of the past decade, CurrencyXchanger POS (CXR-POS) has become one of the most popular independent software for Foreign Exchange in the market. CXR-POS is ideal for MSB (Money Service Businesses) that are cash focused.

Its simplicity and flexibility is only second to none. If you are thinking of automating your bureau de change, you have come to the right place. If you are starting your business and only deal with cash, travelers checks as the most common form of payment, then the POS is going to change your business forever.

If you have a small kiosk or store that offers cash-based foreign currency exchanger to your customers then CurrencyXchanger POS Edition is the perfect match for your business. This edition is better suited for currency exchange outlets in airports, travel agencies, hotels, motels, in-store exchange outlets, and other businesses that offer currency exchange services to their customer and accept cash and travellers checks as the only form of payment.

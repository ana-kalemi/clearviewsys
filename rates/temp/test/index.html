CXR Pro Edition

CurrencyXchanger Pro (CXR-Pro) offers all the features of the Business Edition plus some specific online remittance functionality. CXR-Pro is made for foreign exchange businesses who offer Money Remittance service through their own network of international remittance agents.


The eWire module allows agents to send and retrieve money transfer in form of an electronic payment order (eWire). This funtionality makes it possible for agents to track their payments.

Digital Currency Rate Board

A digital currency rate board/panel is used to display foreign exchange rates an LCD or LED monitor. These boards can also be used to display flight price information, interest rates and lottery results.

The Digital Currency Rate board is a piece of software developed using Javascript, CSS and HTML 5 allowing maximum flexibility and easy customization. We can customize the basic look and feel (colours, fonts, placing your logo) for you.
